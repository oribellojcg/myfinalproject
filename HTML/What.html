What is a Smile?

Smiling is a natural response to something funny, happy, or enjoyable.
A smile is an easily accessible communication tool.
A smile is a contactless hug.

A smile is a facial expression which includes contraction of muscles and corner of eyes.BUT a smile is not just a facial contraction.
A SMILE IS THE UNIVERSAL POSITIVE GESTURE!
The smile is an expression to show feelings, it is the way to communicate with others. It is an emotion, a feeling which creates happiness & unity. A smile is a conveying gesture to show thoughts such as joy, pleasure, amusement, merriment, and enjoyment. A smile shows agreement.
A SMILE MAKES THE PRESENCE OF ANOTHER PERSON COUNT!
